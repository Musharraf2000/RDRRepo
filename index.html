Arthur Morgan Outlaw, survivor, and a complicated man from Red Dead Redemption 2.

Leader, mentor, and gunslinger of the Van der Linde gang. Arthur’s story is one of loyalty, loss, and the search for a better self in a world that’s disappearing.

About Arthur

Raised on the road and forged by the gang he called family, Arthur balances ruthless practicality with flashes of compassion. He’s a sketcher, a hunter, a horseman—and a man learning what honor means to him.

Role

Senior Enforcer, Van der Linde

Dutch’s right hand and often the voice of reason when plans go sideways.

Traits
  • Pragmatic & fiercely loyal
  • Skilled tracker & marksman
  • Surprisingly thoughtful and artistic
Abilities
  • Dead Eye marksmanship
  • Expert horsemanship
  • Survival & crafting in the wild
